About Us

Ingwan'Elihle expertise delivering modern, compliant solutions

Ingwan'Elihle Health & Safety Management Consultants is a built environment firm specialising in health & safety management, event safety management and occupational safety. Our strength lies in a unique blend of scientific insight, global exposure, structured project management, and technology-driven practices.

Our company is founded and led by a Director with a Bachelor’s degree in Microbiology & Biochemistry, a postgraduate certificate, a diploma in Safety Management, and an advanced certificate in Project Management. Her extensive experience spans international projects in Asia, advanced project management methodologies, microbiological science, and emerging software engineering capabilities—enabling us to deliver modern, compliant, and efficient solutions across the built environment
